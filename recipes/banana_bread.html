Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 2-3 medium to large 'really' ripe bananas
  2. 76g melted, (un)salted, butter
  3. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  4. 1 pinch of salt
  5. 200g cane sugar
  6. 1 large, beaten, egg
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla
  8. 205g flour

Preparation

Preheat oven and prepare baking pan:

Preheat oven to 180°C, and place baking paper in an 8 x 4-inch bread pan.

Mash the bananas and add the butter:

Mash, with fork, ripe bananas and sugar until batter is completely smooth.
Stir melted butter into the mashed bananas

Mix in the remaining ingredients:

Mix in baking powder and salt.
Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
Mix in the flour

Fill the pan and up in oven:

Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan
Bake for 55 to 65 minutes at 180°C, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Cool and serve:

Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes.
Remove bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving.
