Egg Nog Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 6 large egg yolks
  2. 1/2cup granulated sugar
  3. 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  4. 2 cups milk
  5. 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg (grounded)
  6. 1 pinch of salt
  7. 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (bourbon)
  8. grounded cinnamon for topping
  9. Â¼ appleton estate Jamaican rum or any good rum

Preparation

Whisk egg yolks and sugar together until creamy.

combine the cream, milk, nutmeg and salt in a pot, at medium heat. Stir often until mixture simmers

Add a spoonful of simmering milk to egg mixture while whisking. Repeat over and over, a spoonful at a time

With most of the milk added to the eggs, pour the mixture back into the pot

Whisk until mixture slightly thickens. It will get thicker the cooler it gets.

Remove from pot and stir in vanilla, and alcohol (to your taste)

Pour eggnog through a fine mesh strainer into a pitcher and cover with plastic wrapping

Refrigerate until nicely chilled

Drink up

Serve with a dash of nutmeg

Keep in Refrigerator (good for up to 1 week)
