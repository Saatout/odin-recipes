Potato Pudding Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 900g peeled and and cut into squares, sweet potatoes
  2. 415ml coconut milk
  3. 200g cane sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 1 teaspoon (fresh) grated ginger
  6. 2 1/4 cups flour
  7. 1 teaspoon grounded cinnamon
  8. grounded cinnamon for topping
  9. 1grounded nutmeg
  10. pinch of salt

Preparation

preheat oven to 180C line a 9-inch baking pan with baking paper

pureé sweet potato chunks and coconut milk in blender

pour pureé into a bowl and stir in remaining ingredients by hand

Pour batter into baking pan and place in oven for circa 1 hour and 10 minutes

check with toothpick, regularly, after an hour if the pudding is baked (toothpick should come out dry)

Eat up

let pudding cool before serving.

enjoy with egg nog
